--- layout: page title: Earthquake player headincludes: - olhead.html --- What is this?

A tool to replay earthquake series from USGS earthquake feeds, catalog search, EMSC catalog search, user specified GeoJSON URLs and files, or included files featuring the July 2019 Ridgecrest quakes.
Built using OpenLayers. Data courtesy USGS. Bugs by me.

How do I use it?

Scroll down to the controls, pick your poison and hit Play. Using Chrome or Firefox is suggested.

I wish it did something different. Can I modify it?

Yep. It's a mess... caveat forker.
